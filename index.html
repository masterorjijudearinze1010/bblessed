    Welcome to BBlessed Foundation

    Over the past decades, BBlessed endowment has grown to a value of $5.9 billion, after charitable activities and costs. With this strong financial base, we have built a growing portfolio of charitable grants with a value of over $1.6 billion. In the 2019 financial year, grant disbursements totalled $269 million

    About Us Make Donation

    Who We Are

    BBlessed foundation is the world's largest philanthropy that focuses specifically on improving children's lives. CIFF works with a wide range of partners, and areas of work include maternal and child health, adolescent sexual health, nutrition, education, deworming, tackling child slavery and exploitation, increasing opportunities for girls and young women, and supporting smart ways to slow down and stop climate change

    Our Projects Make Donation

    Our History

    Over the past 80 years we have invested in innovative ideas, visionary individuals, and frontline institutions advancing human dignity around the world. Our social justice mission and vision have guided us through transformations in the foundation, the communities we serve, and the world at large.
